Troubleshooting AR Issues

Having trouble with the AR feature? Here are some common reasons and solutions:

  • Ensure your device supports AR and has the required sensors like a camera and gyroscope.
  • Make sure your browser has permission to access your camera.
  • Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser if the AR view doesn't load.
  • Update your browser to the latest version for better compatibility.
  • Check your internet connection; You made need an active connection for the software to work correctly.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies in case of persistent issues.
  • Disable any browser extensions that might block AR scripts or functionalities.
  • Verify your environment has adequate lighting for the camera to function well.
  • Ensure the marker used for AR is correct and correctly aligned and visible.
